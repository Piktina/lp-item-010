Xanh SM Bike service;

Total fee to be paid includes: fares displayed on the application and surcharges (if any), of which:

1. Fares displayed on the application include:

  • Opening fare for the first 2km: 13,800 VND
  • From the third km onwards: 4,800 VND/km

*Flexible pricing is applied subject to demand increases based on area and time of day. The above fares do not include trip insurance.

2. Other surcharges (may) include:

  • Holiday surcharge: 5,000 VND/trip - applied according to State holidays.
  • Night surcharge: 10,000 VND/trip - applicable from 10:00 pm to 5:59 am. Surcharge for change of destination: 5,000 VND/time (maximum 01 time) - unlimited distance.
  • Surchage for additional destination: 5,000 VND/stop- maximum 01 additional stop.

* The final price displayed on the application is the amount you need to pay for using the service after deducting promotions/discounts (if applicable), inclusive of VAT according to Resolution 406.

* Xanh SM Bike service is applicable for maximum 01 adult and 01 child under 14 years old per trip.
